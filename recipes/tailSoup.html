Red Dragon Tail Soup

Description

The tail of a Red Dragon is a prized delicacy. It’s tough, but when cooked in a soup, the meat becomes tender and infuses the broth with a rich, smoky flavor.

Ingredients

  • Red Dragon Tail
  • Flame Bat Fat
  • Fire Tubers
  • Golden Moss Potatoes
  • Dungeon Rock Salt
  • Fire Lizard Spice
  • charred Pheonix Feathers

Steps

  1. Defeat a Red Dragon in the depths of the dungeon and carefully cut off the tail. The tail meat is dense and rich in flavor.
  2. In a cauldron, render flame bat fat and sear chunks of Red Dragon Tail until browned.
  3. Add dungeon root vegetables like fire tubers and golden moss potatoes for added texture.
  4. Pour in water from the dungeon’s magma-heated springs and simmer the soup slowly.
  5. Season with dungeon rock salt, fire lizard spice, and charred phoenix feathers to enhance the smoky flavor.
  6. Simmer for several hours until the meat is tender and the broth is infused with dragon essence.
  7. Serve hot with chunks of dragon meat and vegetables floating in the rich broth. This dish replenishes stamina and provides resistance to fire-based attacks.
